# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in Jojo OS a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other people

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling or deliberate disruption
- Publishing others’ private information without consent
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome advances
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer via the contact on the GitHub profile, or through GitHub’s reporting tools. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, and contributors that violate this Code of Conduct.
